CIVIL 360 LLC is a Civil Engineering Firm that specializes in integrated planning, engineering, and design services for both private and public sector clients.

Our team provides exceptional and expert client and civil engineering services supported and backed with a strong local knowledge and perspective, and an extensive experience in communicating with the local governing agencies.

Our ability to provide comprehensive civil engineering services is efficient in terms of time, cost and ability to coordinate between services. Our goal with every project is to provide proactive communication with both our clients and the project team, actively reducing the likelihood of delays caused by redundancy of effort and/or misunderstandings between team members.
