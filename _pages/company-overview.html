Who We Are

CIVIL 360 LLC is based in Las Vegas, Nevada, and is locally owned and operated. The company offers a team of knowledgeable, experienced and trustworthy staff to provide integrated planning, engineering, and design services for both private and public sector clients.

Our Philosophy

Relationships are the most important part of our business and we believe that responsive service, a quality product and timely deliverables are the basis for mutually beneficial relationships and continued future success.

CIVIL 360 will always look out for what is best for our clients and will seek to engage municipal staff, as needed, in seeking out creative solutions to create a win-win situation for all parties involved.

Our Founder

Jennifer L. Escobedo, P.E.
Principal

Ms. Escobedo grew up in New Jersey, attended Widener University and graduated with her B.S. degree in Civil Engineering. She then moved to Indiana where she attended graduate school at Purdue University and graduated with her M.S. degree in Civil Engineering in 1996.

After graduation in 1996, Ms. Escobedo started working at a local firm in Indianapolis as a junior roadway engineer. In 2000 she was offered a position in Las Vegas, joining one of the companies working on the Hoover Dam Bypass project. In 2002 she began working in Land Development, and that is when her passion for civil engineering started to grow.

Ms. Escobedo most recently served in the capacity of Regional Manager for a national engineering firm where she had an opportunity to develop and cultivate relationships beyond her expectations. Her passion for civil engineering, along with the support she has received from some very influential members in the industry, have led to the establishment of CIVIL 360 LLC.
